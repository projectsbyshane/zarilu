Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ZARILU manages personal information in accordance with Australian privacy standards.

1. Collection of Information

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when enquiring about our products. This is strictly limited to:

  • Name
  • Phone Number

We do not collect or store addresses, age, or financial data.

2. Use of Information

Your details are used solely to communicate with you regarding your enquiries and custom orders for our candles and fragrances. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. No Online Transactions

ZARILU is currently a showcase landing page. There is no shopping cart, payment gateway, or method to purchase items directly through this website.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure your personal information is stored securely and protected from unauthorised access or misuse.

5. Access and Correction

Under Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you or request its deletion at any time.

6. Contact

For any questions regarding your privacy, please contact us via the details provided on our homepage.
